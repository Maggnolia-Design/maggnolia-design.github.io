"Why I'm a Designer"

In this "personal design catalogue," I explore several things that I "collect" and people/collections that I enjoy—including postcards, teapots, sneakers, and Japanese envelopes known as Pochi Bukuro. It called for the research of the history of various topics and people.

The project also touched on design and crafting skills, including paper selection and book binding. Through several iterations, I repeatedly practiced designing, coptic stitching, photographing, crafting, and more. Thus, my attention to detail and creativity improved as well.
