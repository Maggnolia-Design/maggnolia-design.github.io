Why I'm a Designer

Book and binding   |   Misc. materials

This assignment required me to identify and catalogue items that I personally "collect," as well as people and collections which affect who I am as a designer. I was also asked to lay out the text and bind the book in a creative way. The result was a 60 page, coptic stitch booklet with photographs and pocketed pages, alongside information about each piece's history, both personal and general.
