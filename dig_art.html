Fine and Digital Art

Oil on canvas, Photoshop, and Illustrator

I began oil painting at an early age, and learned by studying the landscapes, still lives, and portraitures of Renaissance artists. My interest in portraiture eventually transcended oil and pencil to include digital art as well.

The following collection showcases a range of styles and media. This is the artistic foundation which facilitated my eventual transition into digital art and design.
