The Journey Unravels

Journey Map   |   InDesign and Illustrator

This information design project from fall 2018 details how I travel between my Boston residency to my parent's house and to an international destination. We were asked to include at least 10 pieces of infomation. I presented the project as a scroll which unravels to roughly 7.5 feet.
