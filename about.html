About Me

Carrier pigeons are the preferred means of contact; however, e-mails and phone calls are also encouraged.

Hey, I’m Maggie! I am an Interaction Design major at Northeastern University with a passion for problem-solving and learning new things. With the right people and attitude, the creative process is full of excitement.

My current learning goal is web design. Aside from building my skills in html and css through projects such as the one you see before you, I hope to learn more about bootstrap, Sass, and jQuery. Above all, I want to create design solutions that are beautiful and meaningful.

As a fourth year, I've had the pleasure of designing advertisements and branding elements for various events in my community, including a regional conference on APPI identity and a conference on science and engineering. The co-founder and co-president of a classical and ethnic chinese dance troupe, I do branding and social media marketing for my own organization as well. I am pursuing a minor in computer science as well.
