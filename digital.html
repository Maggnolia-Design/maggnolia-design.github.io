Wedha's Pop Art Portrait

Wedha's Pop Art Portraits (WPAPs) refer to an Indonesian pop art genre in which the artists traces multi-colored polygons from a photographic portrait. The first pieces has a striking color contrast between portrait and background. The second portrait depicts a moment of mirth. The bright colors on the face mirrors the glowing expression, and the darker, less saturated hues on the hair, neck, and shirt serve as contrast.
