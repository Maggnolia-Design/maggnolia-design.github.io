ownerIQ Marketing Icons

Iconography | Illustrator

The following pieces are part of a 2018 revamp of the ownerIQ marketing materials. These fixed-color icons work alongside the existing monochromatic line icons to express the owneerIQ identity and values—transparency and dynamism.

Meticulous attention to color palette, strokes, and alignment were essential to creating consistency and pixel-perfection within each icon. Compared to the original line icons, these also allowed for a more dynamic use of color and shape.
