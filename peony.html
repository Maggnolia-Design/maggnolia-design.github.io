The Peony Pavilion

Poster and Pamphlet | Photoshop, Illustrator and Indesign

The Peony Pavilion is a famous Ming Dynasty Kunqu opera. The poster and pamphlet's contemporary style pairs well with its highlighting of key visual elements distinctive to traditional Chinese opera.

For example, Kunqu opera actors use black and pink paint on a white face to accentuate facial features. They also dawn heavily ornamented headdresses and curls of hair on the forehead.
