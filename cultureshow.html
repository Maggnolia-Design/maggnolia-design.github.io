Cultural Showcase Event

Poster | Print, Digital

The 2015 Chinese Cultural Showcase was the Northeastern Chinese Student Association's first annual cultural showcase. Organized around the time of the lunar new year, the event served to acknowledge the holiday as well as showcase NUCSA's premiere dragon dance team and headliner MC Jin.

The main imagery draws attention to the two featured performances of the night. The cheery red color is considered extremely auspicious and symbolic in Chinese culture.

The dragon image, adapted from another source, is in the typical traditional style. This advertisement also appeared in a digital banner version for social media.

Coded by Maggie Zhang.
