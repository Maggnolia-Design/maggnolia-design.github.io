Eon Dance Troupe

Identity | Photoshop and Illustrator

Eon Dance Troupe is an Asian dance group which I co-founded and branded. We bring tradition and culture to young contemporary audiences. The logo expresses Eon's name and mission in a merging of traditional and modern aesthetics.

The handwritten "eon" mimics the elegant, rounded aesthetic of classical Chinese dance — our main competence. The monochrome aesthetic can be easily adapted for different situations, including promoting new dances each semester.
