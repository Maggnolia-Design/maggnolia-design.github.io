Eon Dance Troupe

Identity | Print, Digital

Eon Dance Troupe is an Asian dance group founded by myself and a friend at school. We aim to bring tradition and culture to contemporary audiences.

The Brand

The brand design reflects Eon's name and mission in an elegant merging of traditional and modern aesthetics.

The handwritten "eon" mimics the flowing nature of dance, and in particular, the long silk fans featured in our debut performance.

Drafts and Usage

The austere and monochrome aesthetic can be easily adapted for different situations; for example, they can be laid over photographs of different dances

Towards the bottom are earlier drafts of the logo; although less legible and limber, they also carry a geometric elegance.
