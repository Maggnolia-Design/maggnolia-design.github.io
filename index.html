About Me!

Hey, I’m Maggie! I'm a Interaction Design major at Northeastern University with a passion for problem solving and learning new things. I get energy from using my skills to impact others and creating something both beautiful and meaningful. It's also thrilling to know that with the right people and right attitude, sparks of ideas can catch fire.

My current fixation is web design -- aside from building my skills in html and css, I enjoy teaching myself bootstrap, Sass, and jQuery, as well as following the newest trends. To translate a page from wireframe to code is quite a captivating process!

As a third year student, I've had the pleasure of designing advertisements and branding elements for various events in my community, including a regional conference on APPI identity and another on creativity and collaboration in science and engineering. The co-founder and co-president of a classical and ethnic chinese dance troupe, I do branding and social media marketing for my own organization as well. When I'm not designing, coding, or dancing, you may catch me dancing hip-hop, running cross country, or sketching.
