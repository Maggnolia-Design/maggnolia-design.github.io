About Me!

Hey, I’m Maggie! I’m a graphic designer in Boston, MA, and a Candidate for the Bachelor of Fine Arts with a Concentration in Interaction at Northeastern University. I like to balance dependability and imagination in all aspects of my life, and get a thrill out of finding beauty in chaos.

Often times, I do this through the design of advertisements and branding projects for various cultural organizations in my community. In 2014, I collaborated with university students from Northeastern, Boston University, and Harvard as a member of the East Coast Asian American Student Union to promote racial awareness and publicize a regional conference on APPI identity. Since then, various cultural clubs at Northeastern have reached out to me with subsequent branding and marketing projects.

My most recent endeavor — and matter of much personal investment and fulfillment — is Eon Dance Troupe. Co-founded and ran by myself and a friend, Eon is a classical, ethnic, and fusion Chinese dance group that teaches and performs dances with members of all experience levels. Along with promoting Eon through social media and generating our brand through identities designs, photography, and video, I share logistical responsibilities with my partner such as organizing practices and establishing relationships with other clubs.

In my free time, I also enjoy sports, modern dance, and web design; pushing boundaries, learning something new, and solving problems is a delightful and never-ending process!
