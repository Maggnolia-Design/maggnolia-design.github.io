Typographic Play

Book cover design

To create this cover to "Three Men in a Boat" by Jerome K. Jerome, I created lazer-cutouts of the typography and floated the text in water. The illusion of sinking and floating were created with layering and masking of the photographs in Photoshop.

Chinese Calligraphy

Chinese is a pictorial written system. I correlated the forms of the characters with their meaning, emphasizing the pictoral foundation of written Chinese language.
