Digital Arts

portraiture   |  Photoshop and illustrator

I began oil painting at an early age, and learned by studying the landscapes and portraits of Renaissance artists. After more exposure, I developed my own original works. This artistic foundation facilitated my eventual transition into digital art and design.

My interest in portraiture transcended oil and pencil to include digital art as well. Wedha's Pop Art Portraiture (WPAP) refers to an art trend originating in Indonesia. Characterized by brightly colored polygons, it requires a careful balance of shapes, colors, and contrast.
